How much take?

(1)The general recommendation for most adults is to limit coffee consumption to about 3-4 cups per day, which typically equates to around 300-400 mg of caffeine. This amount is generally considered safe and can provide the benefits of coffee without leading to adverse effects like jitteriness, insomnia, or digestive issues.
(2)However, caffeine sensitivity varies from person to person, so it’s important to listen to your body and adjust your intake accordingly.

How to make?

(1)Fill the coffee maker's water reservoir with the desired amount of water. Place a coffee filter in the basket and add ground coffee (usually 1-2 tablespoons per 6 ounces of water). Pour the coffee into a cup and enjoy!
(2) Boil water and let it cool slightly (to about 200°F or 93°C). Add 1-2 tablespoons of coarsely ground coffee per 6 ounces of water to the French press.

What is coffee?

Coffee is a popular beverage made from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of the Coffea plant. It is enjoyed worldwide for its stimulating effects, primarily due to its caffeine content, which can help increase alertness and reduce fatigue.
